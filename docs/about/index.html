Why This Show Exists

I am Rico, and if you have ever sat down at a poker table and felt that mix of adrenaline, curiosity, and quiet calculation, you are in the right place.

Poker Tales From Across the Table is my way of sharing the psychology, humor, tension, and humanity that make poker unforgettable.

What You Will Find Here

These stories go beyond strategy charts and hand histories. Each episode explores the people behind the cards: how they think, how they tilt, and how they reveal more than they realize.

Voice and Style

The Poker Archetype Universe blends storytelling with table psychology. The Eagle, the Elephant, the Fish, and the Tasmanian Devil are not just characters; they are patterns you can spot in real games.
