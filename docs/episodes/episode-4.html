Summary

"Chip and a Chair" follows Rico—the eagle, storyteller and analyzer—as he relives a defining moment from a massive No‑Limit Hold'em tournament at the legendary Commerce Casino. From one chip to a playable stack, Rico breaks down hand reading and table image manipulation in this masterclass on short-stack strategy and opponent profiling.

When reduced to a single chip in a room full of killers, survival isn't about shoving any two cards—it's about understanding who you're up against. This episode blends survival, psychology, and the craft of hand reading through a cinematic retelling of poker at its most intense.
